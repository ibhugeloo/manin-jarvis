# Getting stronger

The goal is to add lean mass without losing the cardio base I get from trail
running and badminton. That means eating in a slight surplus and training with
progressive overload a few times a week.

## Routine

Compound lifts first, accessories second. Trail runs on the weekend for
endurance. Sleep is the non-negotiable input — strength stalls without it.

## Tracking

I log bodyweight weekly rather than daily to smooth out noise, and I judge
progress over months, not days.
