# How I size positions

My long-term capital is split between a tax-advantaged equity wrapper and a
regular brokerage account. The wrapper holds broad index trackers; the
brokerage account holds a concentrated set of individual companies I want to
own for years.

## Weighting

I lean toward quality businesses with durable free cash flow and consistent
share buybacks. Position weight reflects conviction, not the headline ticker
count. I rebalance rarely and avoid reacting to short-term price noise.

## What I avoid

Leverage, options for speculation, and chasing whatever is momentarily popular.
Patience is the edge.
