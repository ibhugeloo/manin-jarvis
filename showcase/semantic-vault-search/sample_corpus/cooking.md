# Everyday dishes

A pot of rice and a fragrant tomato-and-onion base carry most weeknight meals.
I cook island-style: warm spices, slow-simmered sauces, a squeeze of citrus at
the end. No beef, no pork.

## Pantry staples

Ginger, garlic, turmeric, thyme, and plenty of chilli. Sparkling water is
always in the fridge. Pickled vegetables and a fresh chutney brighten an
otherwise simple plate.

## Weekend project

Slow-braised lentils with smoked spices, finished with coriander, served over
rice. Cheap, filling, and it tastes better the next day.
