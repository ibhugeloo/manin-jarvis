# How I like to travel

I favour Southeast and East Asia. A wheeled suitcase, not a backpack. Hotels
in the comfortable mid-range rather than hostels. Simple food over fine dining.

## Motion sickness

Long road transfers are hard for me, so I plan shorter legs and keep medication
on hand. I avoid boats and small aircraft where I can.

## What I skip

Crowded nightlife and water-based activities. I would rather hike a quiet trail
in the morning than queue for a packed attraction.
